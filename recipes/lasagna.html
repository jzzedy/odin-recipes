Lasagna Recipe

A baked Italian-American dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta, meat or vegetables, sauce (often tomato-based or a béchamel), and cheese, typically mozzarella, ricotta, or Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Meat
  • Nestle: All Purpose Cream
  • Cheese
  • Magic Sarap
  • Flour
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a pot and pour water. Simmer for 30mins. Strain and set aside to cool. Reserve broth.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion in butter for 2mins. Add mushroom and cook for another 2mins.
  3. Add flour and cook for 1min. Stir in reserved broth and NESTLÉ All Purpose Cream. Simmer for 2mins. Season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Set aside.
  4. Shred chicken and mix in the prepared sauce. Set aside.
  5. Boil lasagna sheets in water according to package direction. Drain and set aside.
  6. Spread sauce at the bottom of a baking dish. Place a layer of lasagna. Repeat until all pasta and sauce are layered in the baking dish.
  7. Grate cheese and mix with parsley. Spread evenly on top of the dish. Bake until golden brown. Serve warm.
