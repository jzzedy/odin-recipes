Ginataang Gabi

Ginataan Recipe

Ginataang Gabi, also known as Laing, primarily features taro leaves and stems (gabi) cooked in coconut milk, along with various spices and aromatics.

Ingredients

  • Gabi(Taro)
  • Coconut Milk
  • Aromatics
  • Chili
  • Shrimp Paste

Steps

  1. Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger in oil until fragrant, then add shrimp paste.
  2. Pour in coconut milk and add chilies, then let it simmer.
  3. Add the gabi (taro) leaves on top without stirring and cover the pot.
  4. Once the leaves are wilted, gently press them down into the coconut milk and simmer until tender.
  5. Add thick coconut milk, cook until the sauce thickens and oil surfaces, then serve.
